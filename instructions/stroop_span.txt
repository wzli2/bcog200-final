This is the Stroop-span test. You will first be presented with a row of two colored words. After an amount of time, a text box will appear where you will type each word in order, seperated by a single space. If you answer correctly, another row with one more word will appear. Answer again and repeat this process until you answer incorrectly. The possible words are: "Red", "green", "blue", "yellow", "orange", "purple", "pink", and "black. Good luck!
